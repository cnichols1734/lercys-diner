About Lercy's

Lercy's Diner West is the kind of place that doesn't exist enough anymore — a real, honest, home-cooking restaurant in Mont Belvieu where the owner is behind the counter and every plate is made the way your grandmother would have done it.

Lercy runs the kitchen himself. He's there. He's cooking. He's making sure it's right. It's not fancy. It's not trying to be. It's just really, really good food served by people who care.
